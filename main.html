Maldives - A Tropical Paradise


The Maldives, known for its crystal clear turquoise waters, white sandy beaches, and luxurious resorts, is a tropical haven located in the Indian Ocean. With its serene ambiance and breathtaking natural beauty, it's an ideal destination for relaxation and indulgence.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Sun Island - Perfect for Water Sports Enthusiasts
  2. Male - A Vibrant Capital with Rich Cultural Heritage
  3. Maafushi - Exquisite Local Island Experience
  4. Biyadhoo Island - Pristine Beaches and Diving Opportunities
  5. Hulhumale - Seamless Blend of Urban and Natural Charms

Must-Try Foods

  • Mas Huni - A Traditional Maldivian Breakfast Dish
  • Fihunu Mas - Spicy Grilled Fish Delight
  • Garudhiya - Fragrant Fish Soup with Rice
  • Bis Keemiya - Savory Pastry Filled with Tuna
  • Boshi Mashuni - Refreshing Salad with Coconut and Mango
